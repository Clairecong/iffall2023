Drenched

Artist: Wanting Qu

Album: Everything in the World(2012)

Genre: Pop

"Since I have returned to China and my hometown, of course, I want to sing a song with some Chinese characteristics. 'Drenched' was created by me at the end of last year. Some friends felt that the melody had a Chinese charm. It had been sung in many places around the world before, and I also hope that it can receive recognition from the people of my hometown."
