Mission

Expedia Group is a global travel platform with a mission to power travel for everyone, everywhere. The company's vision is rooted in the belief that travel is a force for good, fostering stronger connections, expanded horizons, and bridging divides across the globe​​​​. Expedia Group is committed to making travel accessible and aims to open the travel ecosystem to people globally. By leveraging their platform, they strive to cultivate a more inclusive and sustainable travel industry​.

We Believe Travel is a Force for Good

Sustainable Future

Expedia Group's sustainability approach, Open World™, is focused on three pillars: providing access for underserved travelers, economic advancement for communities underrepresented in the travel industry, and fostering a more prosperous planet. They aim to democratize the travel business, ensuring widespread benefits from the industry's growth and supporting the success of local businesses. Additionally, Expedia is committed to removing barriers for a more inclusive travel experience and is advancing equity across the industry. Their sustainability efforts include promoting sustainable travel options and striving to achieve Net Zero emissions across operations by 2040, indicating a robust commitment to environmental and community sustainability​.

They focus on strengthening economic opportunities, particularly for local and small businesses, and diversifying the business of travel to improve representation for underrepresented travelers.

In terms of inclusive pathways, Expedia Group is dedicated to removing barriers to travel, making it more accessible to all. They aim to enhance travel experiences for underserved travelers and advance travel equity by addressing gaps in the industry.

For a prosperous planet, the company is driving innovation towards a sustainable travel ecosystem. This includes enabling travelers to choose more sustainable options and committing to a Net Zero future by reducing their operational emissions, aligning with global efforts to mitigate climate change.
